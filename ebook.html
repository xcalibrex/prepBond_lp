Psychometric Test (MSCEIT)

Bond uses the MSCEIT to measure emotional intelligence. It assesses how you perceive, interpret, and respond to emotional context in a professional environment.

MSCEIT Modules

Logic Hack

Successful students don't answer based on intuition. Scoring is standardized on emotional utility. We teach the Mayer-Salovey-Caruso model to categorize intensity.

Match emotions to cognitive tasks. The test rewards understanding which emotional states enhance specific types of thinking—not personal preference.

Predict how emotions evolve and combine. Master the 'blend' questions by learning which emotion pairs produce which complex states.

Choose actions that regulate emotions effectively in professional contexts. Focus on outcomes, not intentions—the MSCEIT scores what works clinically.
