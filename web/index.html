The Truth About the MSCEIT 2

Many students fear this test because they believe it judges who they are as a person. They worry, "What if I'm not empathetic enough? What if I'm too logical?"

Here is the good news: The MSCEIT 2 is not a judgment of your character. It is simply a skill set.

Just like you learned to balance chemical equations, and just like you learned to recognize patterns in UCAT Abstract Reasoning, you can learn the language of Emotional Intelligence. You don't need to change who you are. You just need the right translator.
